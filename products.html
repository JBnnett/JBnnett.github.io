Project Number Three: Developing a Tax Calculator

For my third assignment in programming dyanmic websites, I had to design a calculator that would calculate annual taxes based on income. The calculator would take in employement income and any other income based on what is inputted into the calculator and would calculate the annual taxes the user would have to pay. In addition to calculating the taxes, the program would also subtract a portion of the taxes from the total based on what the user had already put down towards paying thier taxes.
